About the UAV Club

Launched in the fall of 2012, we are one of the newest engineering teams on campus. We are organized into a smaller core team and a larger general team. The core team consists of the officers, some of the first members of MIT UAV, and project leads. The core team meets multiple times a week to handle administrative tasks, plan team activities, develop technical infrastructure, and manage projects. The general team consists of all members who attend weekly build sessions.
